# Privacy Policy  
**KW&SC Unified**  
**Last updated: 17 December 2025**

KW&SC Unified ("**we**", "**us**", or "**our**") operates the **KW&SC Unified** mobile application ("**App**").  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

This policy applies to all users of the App on Android and iOS platforms.

---

## 1. About the App
KW&SC Unified is a digital utility service application that enables users to access water‑related services, request tankers, view billing information, submit support tickets, and manage site registrations through a unified mobile platform.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect only the information needed to operate the App, deliver services, enhance security, and improve user experience.

---

### 2.1 Information You Provide
Depending on your onboarding type (consumer / non-consumer), you may provide:

- Full name  
- CNIC  
- Email address  
- Primary and secondary phone numbers  
- Consumer number (if applicable)  
- Structured address details (area, block, house number, street, etc.)  
- Utility bills or verification documents  
- Support requests, complaints, and feedback  
- OTP verification details (for identity confirmation)

---

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
Collected during App usage:

- Device metadata (device model, OS version, platform type such as Android/iOS)
- App version  
- IP address and user-agent  
- Device ID and device characteristics  
- FCM device token (for notifications)  
- App interaction data (navigation, feature usage)  
- Diagnostic data, crash logs, and performance metrics  

All automatic collection is used strictly for security, analytics, fraud detection, and service improvements.

---

### 2.3 External Provider Data
During onboarding, verification, or billing, data may be retrieved from trusted third-party providers, including:

- Consumer verification data  
- Payment gateway responses  
- OTP service provider logs  
- OCR verification responses  

We store only the **minimal required audit records**, such as:

- External provider name  
- Request/response summary  
- Timestamps  
- Correlation identifiers  

This ensures transparency, traceability, and anti‑corruption auditing.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used for:

- User identity verification (via OTP or documents)  
- Site registration, validation, and membership management  
- Delivering tanker booking and service features  
- Communicating service updates, notifications, and alerts  
- Generating billing, usage history, and support tickets  
- Protecting the platform from misuse or fraud  
- Compliance with auditing and regulatory requirements  
- Improving app performance and user experience  

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not sell** or trade your personal data.

Your data may be shared **only** in the following cases:

- With internal authorized teams for service fulfillment or verification  
- With external service providers strictly for:
  - OTP delivery  
  - OCR and document validation  
  - Hosting, storage, and analytics  
  - Payment processing  
  - Notifications (via FCM)  
- When required by applicable law or legal processes  

All third parties are required to follow strict confidentiality and data‑protection obligations.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security
We use industry‑standard practices to secure your data:

- Encrypted storage and secure communication channels  
- Limited access based on user/employee roles  
- Continuous monitoring and logging of security‑critical events  
- Anti‑corruption audit logs for sensitive operations  

Although no system is 100% secure, we continuously monitor, update, and improve protections.

---

## 6. Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as needed to:

- Provide the requested services  
- Maintain account and operational records  
- Fulfill legal, regulatory, and auditing obligations  

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

### 6.1 Account Deletion
When you request account deletion, your account undergoes a two-stage process:

1. **Soft deletion** – Your account is immediately deactivated and marked for deletion  
2. **Permanent deletion** – After 90 days, all account data is permanently removed from our systems  

During the 90-day grace period, you may request account restoration through official support channels. After permanent deletion, recovery is not possible except for data we are legally required to retain for compliance purposes.

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information  
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data  
- Request deletion of your data (subject to operational/legal requirements)  
- Request information about how your data is processed  

Requests can be made through official support channels.
